Woop recruiting in Sauerbraten

Woop is looking for a couple of well-skilled, IRC-knowledgeable, committed European or American players with strong sportsmanship and a good history in Sauerbraten.

Anyone who believes they are intelligent, honest, friendly and can pass the minimum skill level (one that we think is sufficient to become a Woop), please do not hesitate to ask Drakas for more information (IRC is the place) in the evening. If your skill level passes with certain Woop players, I'll give you a link to an application form to fill in and we will make a decision at our discretion.
