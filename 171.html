Wormux

We can't even remember who introduced us to this silly game: Wormux, a clone of Worms; but as you'd figure - a free one. So far we played about seven hours of the game (Rhubarb/Drakas/Tachyon/Deimos). The current stable is not too stable and the game is unusable, but the SVN versions are prety stable to play with. The game is truly addictive as we already experienced!
