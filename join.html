Joining Woop

Application process:

  1. Fill in the application form. Try to be concise.
  2. E-mail it to raffael at art@woop.us and Cc to drakas@woop.us.
  3. Wait some time.
  4. raffael and Drakas will either Accept or Reject your application.
  5. If Accepted, send us a profile of your own.

We want über skills in Sauerbraten and a leet track record in the community.

You are not required to wear a clan-tag but you cannot be in another clan at the same time.
