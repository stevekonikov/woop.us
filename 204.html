Apparent inactivity

Well, you might have noticed that a few players have not been too active both in IRC, AC and SB.

As much as some of us would like Woop to expand, it is not always possible due to personal circumstances. I hope I explained it well as some players are rather negative about this. Thanks. Drakas.
