News update June '09

Hey guys, you may be wondering why there are no news coming through! We're currently a little busy re-editing the website to make it more content-rich (with statistics), and making major technological improvements in the clan, so bear with us for a few weeks!

Now to the real news! In the past two weeks, we did quite a lot of competitive gaming in Sauerbraten, and recruited two new members in AssaultCube (echo and Sanzo). There also is an AssaultCube v1.0.4 RC1 update available, so make sure you upgrade your client and servers for compatibility!

In the PSL, we managed to qualify for two out of three team games, and Drakas didn't qualify through either insta or FFA. In the games we played this week, tenshi was unsuccessful in defending his title against Liugam (S1 S2 S3 D1 D2 D3), but Majikal successfully defended his against ConMan in FFA (S1 S2 S3 D1 D2 D3). We hope to get back at insta next month. We also beat the RB clan in capture (S1 S2 D1 D2) and will be playing against TC in two weeks in CTF 4on4.

Also, we are looking at inviting more players from Oceania to participate in Sauerbrate games with/against us. So if you're interested, please drop us a line.
