Victory !

To celebrate Christmas properly, w00p is happy to announce their victory during the last tournament of the year : the Christmas cup!
Our all French line-up (Harrek, Honor, Redbull) brought back a glorious victory. To succeed they had to face NF, FD*, and =MyS= during an awesome final match played on ac_power.




"I was very happy to be part of this well-organized tournament, played under christmas spirit, and would like to thanks all the admins and referees, and, obviously, my teamates Honor & Redbull, who helped me to bring gold back home" -Harrek

Congrats Woopyes !
