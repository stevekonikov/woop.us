About

Woop clan is a mature international Sauerbraten and AssaultCube gaming clan founded in October 2006.

We have gained a wealth of success in leading and participating in Sauerbraten, QuakeLive and AssaultCube competitive gaming scenes.

Woop has been led by Drakas, tenshi, Deimos, raffael, Sanzo and Harrek.
