1MA and w00p join forces in ActionCube!

After a discussion, One Man Army have accepted to join forces with woop clan. 1MA clan is an ActionCube clan, so they will pretty much be part of our AC squad, which is extended now. In exchange, our players will play for their team as well in AC matches.

The chosen prefix for games is "1&w|", though both teams' members can choose whichever they prefer - 1MA| or w00p|. Current w00p AC squad stands at:

  1. Drakas (Xan)
  2. wotwot (uberhax)
  3. Rhubarb

The 1MA squad is:
